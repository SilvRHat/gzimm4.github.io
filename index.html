SilvR Ship Studios

Welcome to SilvR Ship Studios, a crew to bring an entirely new style of programming, game design, and entertainment to the world.

The name SilvR Ship derives from the silver traces made with paper and pencil, and the unity of ship’s crew. There is no limit to imagination, nor is there nothing that can't be drawn in a beautiful first concept art. A ship’s crew relies on many roles - navigator, chef, captain, medic, helmsman - and together it can accomplish outstanding achievements with the best adventures.

Gavin Zimmerman
