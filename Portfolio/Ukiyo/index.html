Project::Ukiyo「浮世」

Project::Ukiyo「浮世」brings the (floating) world of Japanese early modern literature into a digital adaptation, in the form of tech art or technical direction. In this adapted work my goal was to reimagine the supernatural forces and Tokugawa setting in a collection of animated films. The concept is inspired from the tale “The Peony Lantern'' in Asai Ryoi’s Hand Puppets, a collection of Kanazoshi literature stories on the supernatural. The story is known to diverge from previous ghost tales which revolve around the Buddist concept of karma and instead depict the supernatural to be driven by immense emotions, especially that of love and jealousy. The main theme in Project::Ukiyo is to portray this once-new style of ghost stories in a technically complex and visually simple 3D animation, where framing, color, texture, animation, sound, and composition reflect the original work.
