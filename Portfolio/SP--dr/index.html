SP//dr

SP//dr is the Spiderman from Earth - 14512, designed by Gerard Way and featured in Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse. The character greatly resonated with me and I not only wanted to program an amazing web swinging game, but bring the pilot-mech-spider trio to life with a game showcasing its unique style and perspective. This is a personal, nonprofit, and fan-created project.
