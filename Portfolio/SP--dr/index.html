About


SP//dr is made up of three components: a pilot, radioactive spider, and mech suit. I want to recreate this character originally designed by Gerard Way in my own design as an experimental video game. Peni Parker and her spider both contribute in operating SP//dr, so I'm exploring the idea of having AI actively influence controls and user input in a way where the player does not feel restricted.

Why


I absolutely love the character Gerard Way came up with, which recently (2018) was featured in Into The Spider-Verse. And I want to be able to play them, so I took on this project.
I saw potential to experiment with very unconventional methods of character controls featuring AI and weighted camera panning.

Also I want to try and outdo insomniac games on their spiderman games, because they're great!
