Setting


Times are troubling in this steampunk-esc era where tech advances simply canâ€™t keep up with resource supply. This has brought about the new trade of a scraper bounty hunter, those who master in salvaging highly-prized materials. These scrapers operate in 3 person teams and are masters social engineering. However, their goal is highly competitive and often requires trickery on other teams. Play with your friends as a scraper team, search the map for clues, and uncover valuable artifacts before anyone else.

Why


I've taken this on as a solo project and am directly working on every aspect of the game from level design to UI/ UX to programming. I'm using this as an opportunity to connect with various developers skilled in various disciplines and better learn their processes. Its a project I'm really excited about and have been thinking of for over a year.
