DICED

Wacky Multiplayer Cooking Sim

Diced

A stylized pizza-making game where 4 players compete for the top score on constructing outrageous pizzas. Players battle in kitchens equipped with stand mixers, cutting boards, stovetops, and a grand pizza oven. Every round a kitchen, theme, and recipe are chosen. Submissions are scored on dough ratios, correct toppings, cooking job, and hidden bonuses. Claiming wins, players can refine their knife skills, speed, and chef-know-how.


Platforms: PC, Mobile, Tablet

Developer: Plated Studio (On Roblox)

Mechanics

The game is dynamic in so many ways - from the recipes to the maps and ingredients.
Players start out in a lobby, where servers can accumulate up to 4 players which then leads into the main gameplay. Recipes and maps are choosen at random or by player-purchases. Rounds last 2.5 minutes where the player attempts to follow the given recipe as close as possible. Each recipe comes with its own set of hidden bonuses, easter eggs, and inspiration expanding on gameplay strategies.
