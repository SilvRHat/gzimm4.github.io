Chaotic Character Animations and Visual

Noir Verscottie

Noir is a character I developed for an art course earlier this year. Since then I've been expanding on the character in her own video game. I want this project to showcase crazy visual components and movement systems which truly give the player a sense of the agility and oddities of being a ghost. And chaotic dynamics came as a perfect tool for achieving that.

About the Project

As part of Chaotic Dynamics, I took on a project to formally define a method and tool of utilizing chaotic attractors for movement and aesthetic systems in games and animations. These methods of chaotic variations are intended for constrained physical systems to behave in a continuous and disorderly way.

Goals / Motivations

  • Achieve a dynamic, fluid, and natural procedural animation through embedding chaos into the system's movement.
  • Utilize the deterministic behaviour to reduce memory requirements for recreating same movement patterns.
